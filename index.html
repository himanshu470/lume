______ABOUT THE TEAM

DELIVERING
VIBRANT NEW
WAYS TO LIVE
IN THE MOST
DYNAMIC
EMERGING CITY
IN AUSTRALIA

Since its launch in 2001, Brisbane-based Devcorp has specialised in creating superior, beautiful & life-enhancing multistory boutique residential developments. Each Devcorp project is purposely designed to enhance the lives of its residents through unique, superior and livable spaces that showcase quality workmanship and the beauty and skill of inspired carpentry.

COX Architecture is a multidisciplinary practice with six Australian and three international studios integrating architecture, planning, urban design and interior design. Cox is internationally renowned for designing buildings that enrich the exprience of space through an ethos based on the principles of structure, craft, art and nature.

Kris Kowalski Architects is a Brisbane-based, international architectural practice with a core philosophy of successfully translating design concept into physical reality. Since 1991, their approach to working in partnership with clients has delivered award winning flexible and responsive designs, ranging from green building designs to master planned developments.

Hutchinson Builders is Australia's largest, privately owned construction company. For over 100 years and five generations, they've placed family values at the core of every structure, every teams, every task - leaving behind a rich history and constructing a bright future ahead. They are known as one of the few 'truly local' national builders with offices across the country.
